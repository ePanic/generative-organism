GENERATIVE ORGANISM

an aquarium of mathematical life

Each creature is a unique mathematical attractor — thousands of points orbiting through equations.

HOW TO USE

  • TYPE — switch current creature to a new type
  • +ADD — spawn additional creature alongside
  • 3D OFF/ON — toggle slow auto-rotate; scroll/pinch to zoom
  • TAP CREATURE — open its controls
  • COLOR — changes the current creature's palette instantly
  • MANDELBROT — double-click anywhere to zoom in; renders progressively
